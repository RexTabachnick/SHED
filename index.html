Why Third Space

Making friends as an

Friendship research shows proximity and repeated interaction are the key ingredients. Our 4-week structure is designed around that science.

When you're doing something together, conversation flows naturally. You bond over shared experience, not forced intros.

Small, intentional groups

Each cohort adds to your network. Do multiple rounds and you'll have a whole new social life within months.
